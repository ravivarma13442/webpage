About Us....

Flavourful BAR n Restaurant Dining In Downtown Toronto

OCA restaurant brings classic Italian dining to downtown Toronto in collaboration with our partner Chef Oliver Glowig, one of Rome's most celebrated chefs who has been awarded numerous Michelin Stars over his career. Experience locally sourced, seasonal ingredients through homemade pastas, fresh seafood and traditional desserts. Complement your dinner with aged selections from the cheese cave. The restaurant experience is elevated with wine dinners, cooking classes, tastings, workshops and other culinary opportunities.
